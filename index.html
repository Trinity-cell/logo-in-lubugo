How the Studio Process Works

1. We Draft & You Preview
We translate your logo into a paper cut-out draft and share images for approval.
2. You Approve
Once approved, you pay a 50% deposit and we begin fabrication.
3. We Fabricate
Your logo is carefully cut from barkcloth, mounted, and framed by hand.
4. You Approve & We Deliver
You review the finished piece, settle the balance, and we arrange courier delivery.
